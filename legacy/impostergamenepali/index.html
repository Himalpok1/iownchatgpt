Khel Kasari Khelney

Objective

Regular khiladi le chor chinchnu parcha. Chor haru le afu normal jasto dekhaunu parchha ra pakdau nahuney pryas garnu.

Setup

  • 3-20 khiladi chaan nu
  • Sabdakoti ra istar chaan nu
  • Game mode chaan nu (Sidha Khel ya Andhakaar)
  • 1 ya 2 chor decide garnus

Gameplay

  • Word Reveal: Device ek-ek jana lai pass garnus. Koi le secret word dekhchha (regular khiladi) ra chor le chor ko message dekhchha
  • Discussion: Sabai jana kura garnus tara word siddhai nabhannu. chor haru le milayera bolna parcha
  • Voting: Sabai jana vote dinchhan kunai ek jana chor ho bhanera
  • Results: chor pakdiyo bhane regular khiladi jitchhan. Pakdiyana bhane chor jit dinchha

🎭 Game Modes

Sidha Khel Mode: chor lai tha huncha ki tini chor hun. Screen ma "Tai ho Chor!" bhanera aauxa. Regular khiladi le secret word dekhchhan.

Andhakaar Mode: Kahile pani thaha hudaina kasko word milcha kasko milena. Sabai lai word matra dekhinchha, indication hudaina. Regular khiladi sabai lai same word aaucha, chors lai farak word. Discussion ma farak word bhayeko kasko ho bhanera find garnu parcha, tara afno word direct bhannu hudaina.

🎯 Jitney Conditions

Sidha Khel Mode: chor vote bhayera bahira gayo bhane regular khiladi jitcha. Gayena bhane chor jitcha.

Andhakaar Mode (1 chor):

  • Different word bhayeko khiladi vote bhayera bahira gayo → Regular khiladi jitcha
  • Regular khiladi bahira gayo → chor jitchas

Andhakaar Mode (2 chors):

  • Duiwota chor (different word wala) bahira gayo → Regular khiladi jitcha
  • Kamalai ek jana regular khiladi bahira gayo → chors jitchas

💡 Tips

  • Bistarai bujhaune kura garnus tara dherai obvious nabannus
  • Dherai confused ya aachama dekhinchha vaney suspicious huncha
  • chors: aru jastai bolnus, sadhai sunera, milera bolnus

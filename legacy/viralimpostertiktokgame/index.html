How to Play

Objective

Regular players must identify the imposter(s). Imposters must blend in and avoid being caught.

Setup

  • Choose 3-20 players
  • Select a word category and difficulty
  • Pick game mode (Classic or In the Dark)
  • Decide on 1 or 2 imposters

Gameplay

  • Word Reveal: Pass device to each player. Click "Reveal Word" to see your word (or imposter message)
  • Discussion: Talk about the word WITHOUT saying it directly. Imposters must blend in
  • Voting: Vote for who you think is the imposter
  • Results: If imposter is caught, regular players win. Otherwise, imposters win

🎭 Game Modes

Classic Mode: Imposters know they're imposters. Screen shows "You are the imposter!" message. Regular players see the secret word.

In the Dark Mode: Nobody knows who has matching words. Everyone just sees a word - no indication of role. Regular players get the same word, imposters get different words. During discussion, figure out who has the different word without directly revealing your word.

🎯 Win Conditions

Classic Mode: If imposter is voted out, regular players win. Otherwise, imposters win.

In the Dark Mode (1 imposter):

  • If player with different word is voted out → Regular players win
  • If regular player is voted out → Imposter wins

In the Dark Mode (2 imposters):

  • If BOTH players with different words are voted out → Regular players win
  • If at least ONE regular player is voted out → Imposters win

💡 Tips

  • Give subtle hints but don't be too obvious
  • Looking too confused or surprised can be suspicious
  • Imposters: Blend in by listening carefully and matching others' energy
